Constant sales training

Sales arguments are dynamic. They constantly change due to new products, new variants, competition etc. So staying up to date requires daily attention and effort – not only at yearly or quarterly product training meetings in remote conference rooms with overloaded agendas.

Selling is individual. The sales process is not. There are tools and methods that, when applied correctly, simply work.

Share what works

Knowledge sharing does not always occur – especially not in highly competitive areas such as a sales organization. By using the competitiveness of your sales force to increase knowledge sharing, everybody wins.

Losing a good sales rep is every sales manager’s nightmare. With BetterSalesman vital sales arguments are stored and easily passed on to new team members in a quick and fun manner.

Reality check

Tracking the actual digestion of your messages by the sales team helps you as a sales manager to fully understand what your people want, like and dislike and how your sales arguments are met by real customers. That’s reality. Now you can deal with it.

Get there before your competitors. Your team will have up to date, razor sharp arguments of new products long before your competitors. New product launches now become fun and traceable. You can provide documentation to HQ and suppliers that your sales people have actually adopted and understood vital sales arguments, and are able to focus on star products during campaigns.

BetterSalesman is an app that the sales reps can use when on the move. It is quick, simple, fun and competitive – just how they like it.
