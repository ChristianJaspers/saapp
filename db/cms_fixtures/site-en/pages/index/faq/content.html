Q: Why should our company use the BetterSalesman software package?

A: The system improves the efficiency of your sales team. All sales arguments are in one place - for them to use to quickly and effectively close the sale. 

 

Q: Do I need to spend lot of time installing and adding content? 

A: No. You can have the BetterSalesman running within 10 minutes! The BetterSalesman app system is made by experienced sales managers who knows the time constraints we have in a sales environment. 

 

Q: Do I need to input the sales arguments myself?

A: No – it is easy to delegate the content creation to product managers, sales assistants or your personal assistant. 

 

Q: Why should my sales Reps use BetterSalesman?

A: Because all salespeople want to be the best. They can be the best by learning to use and develop  sales arguments fast and easily.  

 

Q: How can I convince my sales Reps to use BetterSalesman?

A: Sales people are naturally competitive. At BetterSalesman you can always see the most active sales Reps. This will influence the others to perform better.  

 

Q: Is BetterSalesman a one way communication tool? 

A: No. BetterSalesman is an incentive for the sales Rep to co-create the best argument bank as everybody can add arguments – making these available for the others. 

 

Q: How can I control my Sales Reps?

A: The easy-to-use admin module of BetterSalesman gives you total control and overview of the activities in your team. 

 

Q: How can we as company benefit of BetterSalesman?

A: Your company will stop using inefficient sales arguments and build up a database of the most efficient arguments for closing the sale.
